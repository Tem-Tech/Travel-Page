About wakanda

Wakanda is a fictional country appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics.It is located in sub-Saharan Africa, and is home to the superhero Black Panther. For centuries they have remained in isolation and are now considered the most technologically advanced nation of the planet. Birnin Zana is the capital and largest city. The major languages in Wakanda are Wakandan, Yoruba and Hausa, but due to their advanced education system, the people are fluent in a variety of African and European languages. Wakandans also used Swahili, at least for names.
